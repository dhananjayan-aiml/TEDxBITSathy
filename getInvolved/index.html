About us.

Since its establishment in 2015, TEDxBITsathy has served as a dynamic platform, delving into impactful ideas and pressing issues that resonate with our community and the broader world. From addressing global challenges to celebrating innovation, technology, and cultural diversity, each event has illuminated topics that are current and relevant to our audience. TEDxBITsathy is driven by passionate volunteers committed to spreading transformative ideas, catering to the Curious, Creative, Courageous, and the Connected. Our mission is to influence positive change in the way people perceive and engage with the world around them. As a not-for-profit organization, TEDxBITsathy remains dedicated to consistently delivering on the mission of spreading ideas worth sharing.
